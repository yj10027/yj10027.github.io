Yaonan Jin (金耀楠)

I am a full-time researcher at the Huawei Theoretical Computer Science Lab, led by Prof. Pinyan Lu. My research interests encompass Theoretical Computer Science, with an emphasis on Algorithmic Economics.

Before joining Huawei, I obtained my PhD from Columbia University in 2023, where I was advised by Prof. Xi Chen and Prof. Rocco Servedio. Prior to that, I earned my MPhil from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology under the guidance of Prof. Qi Qi, and my BEng from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
